A Simple Sausage Ragu Recipe

Description

This hearty sausage ragu is rich, cozy, and fast enough for a weeknight. Sweet or spicy Italian sausage simmers with tomatoes and a splash of milk for a silky, mellow sauce that clings beautifully to pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) Italian sausage (sweet or hot), casings removed
  • 1 tablespoon (15ml) extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) dry red wine or low-sodium stock
  • 1 (14oz/400g) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) whole milk or light cream

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage and cook, breaking it up, until browned.
  2. Add onion and cook until softened, 4 to 5 minutes; stir in garlic and cook 30 seconds more./li>
  3. Pour in wine (or stock) and simmer 2 to 3 minutes, scraping up browned bits.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook 20 to 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Stir in milk. Season to taste with salt, black pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes if you like.
  6. Toss with cooked pasta (rigatoni, pappardelle, or orecchiette are great) and serve with grated Parmesan and fresh basil.
